What is a Chinese teapot.

A teapot is a kitchen utensil used for preparation of tea. A typical Chinese teapot is made of clay and isn't that large in size.Depending on the skill of the manufacturer, teapots can vary greatly in terms of the grade of clay used, design, functionality and ornamentation.

This site is focused on three teapot types:

  • Shipiao
  • Xishi
    1. tieguanyin
    2. dahongpao
    3. languiren
